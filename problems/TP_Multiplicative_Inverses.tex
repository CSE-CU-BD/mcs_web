\begin{problem}

%% type: short-answer
%% title: Multiplicative Inverses

What is the multiplicative inverse (mod 7) of 2?
($Reminder$: by definition, your answer must be an integer between
0 and 6.)
\bparts


\ppart


\begin{solution}

4


2 * 4 = 8 \equiv  1 (mod 7)
\end{solution}

\eparts
