%% title: Multiplicative Inverses

What is the multiplicative inverse (mod 7) of 2?  \emph{Reminder}: by
definition, your answer must be an integer between 0 and 6.

\begin{solution}
4, because $2 \cdot 4 = 8 \equiv  1 \pmod 7$.
\end{solution}
