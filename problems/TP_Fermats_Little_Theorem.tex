%% type: short-answer
%% title: Fermat's Little Theorem

What is rem(24^{78}, 79)? (Hint: 79 is prime.
      You should not need to do any calculation!)
\bparts


\ppart


\begin{solution}

1


Fermat's Little Theorem 
            says that $n$^{($p$-1)}
            is congruent to 1 modulo $p$,
            when $p$ is a prime, as 79 is.
\end{solution}
