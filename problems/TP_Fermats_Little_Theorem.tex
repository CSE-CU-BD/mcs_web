\begin{problem}
What is $\rem{24^{78}}{79}$? \hint: 79 is prime.  You should not need
to do any calculation!  

\begin{solution}
1.

Fermat's Little Theorem says that $n^{p-1}$ is congruent to 1 modulo
$p$, when $p$ is a prime, as 79 is.
\end{solution}
