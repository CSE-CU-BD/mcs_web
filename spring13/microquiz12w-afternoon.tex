You roll a fair die twice.

Let Event $A$ be rolling a 1 the first time,

and Event $B$ be rolling a 2 the second time.

\vspace{1cm}

\textbf{Problem 1}

Let our sample space be all possible outcomes

for a two-roll sequence.

What is the size of the sample space?

\vspace{.5cm}

\textbf{Problem 2}

Are $A$ and $B$ disjoint events?

\vspace{.5cm}

\textbf{Problem 3}

What is Pr[$A \cup \bar{B}$]?
